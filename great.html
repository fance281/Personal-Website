THE GREATEST SHOWMAN

Growing up in the early 1800s, P.T. Barnum displays a natural talent for publicity and promotion, selling lottery t ickets by age 12. After trying his hands at various jobs, P.T. turns to show business to indulge his limitless imagination, rising from nothing to create the Barnum & Bailey circus. Featuring catchy musical numbers, exotic performers and daring acrobatic feats, Barnum's mesmerizing spectacle soon takes the world by storm to become the greatest show on Earth.

Director: Michael Gracey
